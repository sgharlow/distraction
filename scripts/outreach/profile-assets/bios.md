## LINKEDIN

The Distraction Index is an independent civic intelligence platform that publishes a weekly, frozen record of U.S. political events scored on two axes: constitutional damage and media hype.

Every week since December 2024, we've tracked, clustered, and scored political events using transparent, auditable algorithms — then frozen the results permanently. No silent edits. No editorial spin.

Our dual scoring system measures:
• A-Score (Constitutional Damage): 7 weighted governance drivers with severity multipliers
• B-Score (Distraction/Hype): Media amplification + strategic manipulation analysis

When an event scores high for damage but low for coverage, our Smokescreen Index flags it.
